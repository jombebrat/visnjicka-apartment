Before You Leave

Check-out checklist

🚭

No smoking reminder: If smoking occurred inside the apartment or building, additional cleaning fees will apply. Thank you for respecting the rules.

🗑️

Garbage: Please take all waste outside and dispose of it in the outdoor containers next to the building. DO NOT USE THE BINS INSIDE THE BUILDING.

1
Collect all your belongings
Check all rooms, wardrobes, bathroom, and the balcony. Don't forget chargers, toiletries, and items left to dry.
2
Take out all garbage
Collect all waste from the kitchen, bathroom, and bedrooms. Dispose of it in the outdoor containers outside the building — not in the building bins.
3
Turn off all lights & appliances
Switch off all lights, the TV, and AC. Make sure the stove and oven are off.
4
Close all windows & doors
Close and lock all windows. Lock the apartment door behind you.
5
Return the key to the lockbox
Place the apartment key back into the lockbox on mailbox nr. 1 and return the entry tag to the lockbox at the building entrance. Leave both lockboxes exactly as you found them — reset the code to 0000 and make sure they are properly closed.
6
That's it — safe travels! 👋
